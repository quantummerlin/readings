About A Moment in Time

Your complete cosmic reading

Why This Exists

A Moment in Time is for anyone who wants an energetic picture of who they may be, what they might be capable of, and how it could coincide with their life later down the track. It is not a rulebook and it is not a prediction. It is a reflective reading designed to be revisited over time.

A Reading You Can Return To

Think of it like a good book you can pick up every few years and appreciate with fresh eyes. Or, if you prefer, a movie you rewatch at a different time in life and notice new details. The story may feel different each time because you have changed.

What You Do With It Is Yours

If you believe in the positivity of the reading, you might use it as a foundation for a great life. If you see it as metaphor or even nonsense, that is also valid. A Moment in Time is meant to offer possibility, not certainty.

Why The Stars?

There has been an ancient obsession with the stars, constellations, and their relationships to one another, and the idea that these patterns might influence us energetically. Whether you see that as meaningful or not, it has inspired human storytelling for thousands of years. A Moment in Time is part of that tradition.

Where We Are

We are based in Melbourne, Victoria, Australia and serve readers everywhere.
