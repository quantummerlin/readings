Last Updated: January 30, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to A Moment in Time ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our astrological and numerological reading services.

By using our website, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

When you use our services, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Name: The name you provide for the reading
  • Date of Birth: Used for astrological and numerological calculations
  • Time of Birth (Optional): For more accurate rising sign calculations
  • Place of Birth (Optional): For location-based astrological data

2.2 Technical Information

We automatically collect certain technical information when you visit our website:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website

2.3 Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Store your last reading information locally
  • Analyze website traffic and usage patterns
  • Serve personalized advertisements

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To Generate Readings: Your birth data is used solely to calculate and generate your personalized astrological and numerological readings
  • To Improve Our Services: We analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • To Display Advertisements: We use Google AdSense to display relevant ads
  • To Communicate: If you contact us, we use your information to respond

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

Your reading information is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. This means:

  • Your data stays on your device
  • We do not store your birth data on our servers
  • You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser data

4.2 Security Measures

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Regular security assessments
  • Limited access to technical data

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies and web beacons to:

  • Serve ads based on your prior visits to our website and other sites
  • Measure advertising effectiveness

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.

5.2 Google Analytics

We may use Google Analytics to understand website traffic and usage patterns. Google Analytics collects anonymous usage data.

5.3 Buy Me a Coffee

We provide a link to Buy Me a Coffee for voluntary support. Their privacy policy applies to any interactions on their platform.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to Object: Object to certain uses of your data

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

6A. Australia (Privacy Act 1988)

We are based in Melbourne, Victoria, Australia and handle personal information in line with the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

  • Access and Correction: You can request access to or correction of your personal information
  • Complaints: If you have a privacy concern, contact us first so we can resolve it

If you are not satisfied with our response, you may contact the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at oaic.gov.au.

6B. Notifiable Data Breaches (NDB) Scheme

If a data breach is likely to result in serious harm, we will notify affected individuals and the OAIC in accordance with the NDB scheme.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. Data Retention

Since we do not store personal data on our servers, retention is controlled by you:

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:
